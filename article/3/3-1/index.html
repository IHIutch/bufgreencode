Zone Descriptions

3.1.1 N-1D Downtown/Regional Hub

  1. Purpose The N-1D zone addresses the core of downtown Buffalo to facilitate development of substantial scale with an intense mix of uses.

  2. Building Types The form requirements of the neighborhood zones are tailored to each building type in accordance with Section 3.2. The following building types are permitted in the N-1D zone:
    • Civic Building
    • Commercial Block
    • Stacked Units
    • Tower

  3. General All development in the N-1D zone must comply with the form standards of Section 3.2, as well as any applicable standards in other sections, including the following:

    Frontage Elements Section 3.3
    Principal Uses Section 6.1
    Accessory Uses Section 6.2
    Temporary Uses Section 6.3
    Landscape Section 7.1
    Fences and Walls Section 7.2
    Stormwater Section 7.3
